CANADA POST

 

 

 

 

 

 

The Problem

 

Canada Post's users currently are having difficulty using Canada Post's online shipment tool. There is a high amount of information and navigation is confusing; the user easily is lost. For a small business owner accessing the shipping section is becoming an arduous process.

 

The Solution

 

By adding the "My Favourites" page, we aim to shorten the online shipping process within the users. Re-organizing navigation and improving the Shipping dashboard would help the user reach their goal.
